My name is Arkadiusz Mierzwinski and this is my portfolio page where I exhibit my photography work that I have collected for the past 3 years of me walking around with my camera. I have visited places all over the United Kingdom as well as Europe to admire the different beautiful locations available to the public outside the main city life. The enjoyment I gain from taking photographs stems from my natural liking to study media related subjects which I have done throughout my entire education.
